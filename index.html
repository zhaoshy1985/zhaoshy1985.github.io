Welcome to my page

Hello everyone! This is Shuyun's page. I am an associate professor at the China University of Geosciences (Wuhan). I am currently lecturing two courses: Fortran Programming and Mesoscale Meteorology. I have published approximately 40 scientific papers, with 15 of them as the first or corresponding author. I also wrote a book Cloudwatching With The Meteorologist. Also I am a contributing author of the IPCC AR6 (chapter 7). I have two cats: Ali and Daodao, and a man. They are all my treasures.
